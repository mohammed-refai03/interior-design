COMMON CONCERNS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the typical timeline for a master bedroom refurbish project? +

Generally, bedroom renovations average 6 to 10 weeks. This covers conceptualization (2 weeks), procurement of bespoke headboards & light setups (4 weeks), and on-site building, paint, and styling (2 weeks).

Do you work with my builders, or do you provide your own contractor team? +

We do both. We are happy to coordinate and supervise your designated builders, providing detailed dwg drawings and hardware models. Alternatively, we can recommend our vetted premium contractors who have crafted with us for years.

How do you handle client approvals for budget modifications? +

All budgets are managed online. If a variation occurs (e.g. material upgrade), a proposal invoice is posted to your Client Dashboard. We never proceed with purchase orders without digital check-off approvals.
