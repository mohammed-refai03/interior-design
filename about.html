OUR BEGINNING

Born From a Shared Obsession With Home

In 2012, after years of helping friends style their homes, Marcus noticed the same problem: beautiful spaces that didn't work for real life. The architectural beauty of a space was often compromised by rigid, uncomfortable furnishings, or functional utility was lost in pursuit of trends.

Today, Stackly Atelier creates homes that balance Scandinavian restraint with the warmth of daily living. We believe your spaces should grow with you, translating your story into permanent structures of comfort, grace, and timeless aesthetic depth.

Marcus Aurelius Founder & Principal Designer
WHAT WE BELIEVE
